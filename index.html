In this research project, I found that users needed a simplified method for managing plant care routines, and a place to find plant care insight. Thus, we found that we need to create a plant application that implemented plant care schedules and reminders for basic plant care using device notifications. Additionally, we implemented a community feature to celebrate plant growth and provide a network for additional plant insight.

Throughout the process, we brainstormed what the application would look like in order to start wireframing.

In this research project, I created a survey via google suite to get to know how plant enthusiasts and admirers care for plants and their interest in plant care and knowledge.

We created a storyboard in order to fully visualize the purpose/use of the Plantae application.

These are results of conducting user tests on the LoFi Prototype.

Once receiving feedback from users, the LoFi Prototype was designed into a high fidelity prototype.

I wrote a case study about this project to show the design process and share more about some of the challenges we overcame.
